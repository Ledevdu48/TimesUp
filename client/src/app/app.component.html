Times Up Online

Welcome

Welcome to Times Up Online, the live game where you and your friends try to guess your custom proposals during various stages!

To Start

Enter a pseudo and a room code which you will share with your friend to play.

To Play

Once you have set up the parameters of the game and the teams, you will be asked to create your proposals for the rest of the game.
In the first step, you can use any word (except the ones in the proposal) to make your team guess the proposal.
In the second step, you must use only one word.
In the third step, you will have to draw something to make your team guess the proposal (no words written).

Chat

You must use another program (like Discord) to chat with your friends during the game.
