Welcome

Welcome to Times' UP Online, the live game
where you and your friends try to guess your custom
proposals during various stages!

TO START

Enter a pseudo and a room code which you will
share with your friend to play.

TO PLAY

Once you have set up the parameters of the game
and the teams, you will be asked to create your
proposals for the rest of the game.
In the first step, you can use any word (except
the ones in the proposal) to make your team guess
the proposal. In the second step, you must use
only one word. In the third step, you will have
to draw something to make your team guess the
proposal (no words written).

TO CHAT

You must use another program (like Discord) to
chat with your friends during the game.
